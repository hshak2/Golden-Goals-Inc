Intro

Welcome to Golden Goals! My name's Moneeb and my goal is to help you take control of your financial future! When I started this business, my mission was simple: to bridge the gap between people wanting to manage their personal finances and those who didn't know where to begin.

Through my own experience and research, I discovered just how many people struggle with basic financial management. In fact, in a survey I conducted, only 33% of people had a budget to track their expenses, but a staggering 70% expressed interest in creating one. This gap inspired me to create a service that would not only help individuals create budgets but also empower them with the tools and knowledge they need to manage their finances confidently. Tracking expenses is one of the biggest pain points for many people and I believe my expertise can help you uncover the pathway to financial freedom! Check out my work and reach out to me for a personal consultation here! I guarantee you won't be disappointed.

Services

We start by meeting with you to understand your expenses, financial goals, and aspirations for the future. From there, we create a personalized budget for 2025 that aligns with your unique situation and helps you stay on track. This is a no- obligation consultation, where our goal is simply to help you lay the foundation for financial success in the coming year.

We provide you with a customized Power Bl dashboard that allows you to track your actual expenses versus your budget in real-time. This interactive dashboard will give you the clarity you need to stay on top of your spending and achieve your goals.

Whether you want to manage your finances on your own or prefer to have us help, we can tailor our level of involvement to your preferences:

  • Hands-On Learning: We'll teach you how to navigate and analyze your dashboard, giving you the skills and knowledge to manage your finances moving forward.
  • Full Service: If you'd rather leave the financial management to us, we'll handle the dashboard for you, providing regular insights and updates to ensure you're staying on track.

Our goal is to empower you, whether you want to take control of your finances or have us manage the details for you.

Stats

Why does Financial Literacy matter? It's no secret that many Americans are facing financial challenges. Consider these eye-opening statistics:

  • 25% of Americans spend more than they earn, which leads to growing debt on credit cards.
  • 34% of Americans have no savings at all, and 69% wouldn't be able to cover an unexpected $1,000 expense.
  • More Americans than ever before are living paycheck to paycheck, struggling to meet financial goals or prepare for the future.

These numbers highlight the urgent need for better financial management. And that's where we come in. We're here to help you take control of your finances, reduce financial stress, and start building a secure future.
